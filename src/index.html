Hello, there!

My name is Scott and I'm a Front-End Developer

I’d been working in the beer industry for well over a decade by the time I was made redundant. It was during the pandemic and everywhere was closed, which made the situation much scarier. Yet as intimidating as it was, it was also an opportunity to reassess my career path, to step out of my comfort zone and try something new.

I retrained as a Software Engineer, balancing an online course with jobs at various breweries in and around Manchester. Eventually, I was offered my first coding job as a WordPress Developer at (appropriately) Sureshot Brewery, redesigning their website. Then, shortly after, I was offered my dream job.

Having studied Film & Media at university, I’d always fantasised about one day working at MediaCity. In September 2024, that fantasy became a reality when I was offered a place on the BBC’s Early Careers Apprenticeship scheme, working with [department]. And that’s where I am now, working as [role] with [team] and studying towards [qualification(s)] .
